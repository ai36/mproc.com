Electronic Brachytherapy

Electronic Brachytherapy (eBx) is a cutting-edge cancer treatment that merges the advantages of external beam radiation therapy and conventional brachytherapy to effectively target skin cancer while minimizing harm to surrounding healthy tissue. This approach allows patients to achieve comparable treatment results without the extended recovery periods or side effects associated with Mohs surgery.

Benefits of Electronic Brachytherapy

  • Pain-free – no surgery, no bleeding, no wound care
  • No scarring – no skin damage, no reconstructive surgery
  • Faster results – only 8-10 ambulatory treatments (10 minutes each)
  • No down time – no interruption of your daily activities

Results of Electronic Brachytherapy

  • Same cure rate as MOHS – supported by published research

Skin conditions treated by Electronic Brachytherapy

  • All Non-Melanoma skin cancers (basal and squamous cell carcinomas) have a Surgery-Free treatment option with the same cure rate
