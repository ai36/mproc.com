Everyone Deserves to Know How Skin Cancer Treatments Compare

Patients who undergo Electronic Brachytherapy (eBx) can achieve same cure rates without the dangerous side effects and lengthy downtimes associated with MOHS surgery.

Electronic
Brachytherapy

  • Cure Rate

    98%

  • Patient Experience

    Painless treatment of skin cancer cells with low energy X-rays and see tumor shrink over time

  • Side Effects

    There is no scarring. Rare side effects include temporary hypopigmentation

  • Treatment Time

    Average 9 treatment sessions (about 10 minutes each) over 4 weeks

  • Journey to Recovery

    There is no pain, bleeding nor wound care to interrupt normal daily activities

  • Non-candidates

    n/a

Mohs
Surgery

  • Cure Rate

    98%

  • Patient Experience

    The skin cancer is surgically removed, resulting in a wound and a lasting scar

  • Side Effects

    In 1 out of 3 cases scarring may need to be fixed with reconstructive surgery

  • Treatment Time

    Size and depth only known after cutting starts and more than 1 surgery may be needed to remove all skin cancer

  • Journey to Recovery

    Surgery wounds require care – changing bandages, avoiding showers and normal activities

  • Non-candidates

    Patients who are not ideal candidates for surgery, including those who take blood thinining medication
