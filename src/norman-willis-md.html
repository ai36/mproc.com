As co-founder of Meridian Park Radiation Oncology Dr. Willis brings over 40 years of experience of multidisciplinary oncology care in the community.

Dr. Willis trained in radiation oncology at UCLA and he is board certified in Radiation Oncology by the American Board of Radiology.

Dr. Willis is passionate about radiation therapy techniques that minimize the exposure of normal tissues to radiation in order to reduce side-effects. He prides himself in taking the time to nurture each patient relationship and to treat his patients like family.
